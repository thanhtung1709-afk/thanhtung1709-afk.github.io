Privacy Policy

This app uses TikTok Login Kit to authenticate users and TikTok Content Posting API to upload videos authorized by the user.

The app may process basic TikTok account information and video files selected by the user for upload.

The app does not sell personal data.

Access tokens are used only to call TikTok APIs authorized by the user.

Users can revoke access from their TikTok account settings.
